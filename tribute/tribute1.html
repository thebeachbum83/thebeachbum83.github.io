"The oldest and strongest emotion of mankind is fear, and the oldest and strongest kind of fear is fear of the unknown."~H.P. Lovecraft
Howard Phillips Lovecraft, born in 1890 in Providence, Rhode Island, was an American author who acheived fame posthumously for his work in horror fiction. Living much of his life in poverty, much of his work went unnoticed by audiences of his time. Considered to be the father of the Cthulu mythos, he is now considered to be one of the most influential authors of his genre in the 20th century.
